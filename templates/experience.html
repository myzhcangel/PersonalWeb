Medical Image Registration Project Remote | Launched by Stanford EE
My very first experience is about image processing and analysis. Launched by Stanford EE, the program asked for approaches for registering or aligning two medical images from different medical equipments of the same part of body. In this project, I explored methods on registration of medical images from different pieces of equipment.
  • Method One: I realized image registration by choosing several pairs of points according to different models and applying them to affine transformation. This method succeeded in realizing the goal.
  • Method Two: I realized image registration by calculating mutual information from two images and looking for the exact positions when the value reached a peak. This method could work automatically and gain higher accuracy.
